Mapping the 18th-Century London Theatrical Community: The Newcomers

The theatrical community of the long eighteenth century includes notable actors, performers, playwrights, librettists, theatrical personnel, artists, and composers. This map, drawing on the Theatronomics dataset, maps the birthplaces of hundreds of British contributors to the vibrant London stage born outside a five-mile radius of London.
